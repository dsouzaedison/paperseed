Our Products

Keeping these principles into consideration our company produces a wide range of eco-friendly, toxin free and sustainable products by either reusing it or recycling it.

Seed Paper

A biodegradable and eco-friendly paper made without harming any trees or effecting the environment which can be used for various purposes like wedding cards, greeting cards, invitations or for promotions.

Paper Pencil

Every year millions of trees are felled to make pencils. At Paper seed, pencils are made completely of used newspapers with no wood, chemicals or plastic. These are smooth & dark in writing, easy to sharpen and lasts way longer.

Agarbatti

These Organic Incense sticks are hand-crafted with flowers which are offered to the religious places. It has the natural fragrance from the flowers and herbs. These are char-coal free and made without artificial scents, colors or preservatives.

Why trust paperseed?

Every product at paper seed that we produce is either reused, recycled and handcrafted without compromising on quality of the end product. As most of our products are hand crafted, it provides job opportunities to the women from rural villages, thus fulfilling our motive of building a better, healthier and greener planet. We use 100% recycled papers mostly, news papers, used office papers and from used note books.
